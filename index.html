Hand-Arm Vibration Syndrome (HAVS) is a serious condition. It affects thousands of workers every year. This is especially true for those in construction, manufacturing, forestry, and maintenance. Prolonged exposure to vibrations from hand-held tools can lead to permanent damage to the blood vessels, nerves, and muscles in the hands and arms.

Compliance with safety standards, such as the Control of Vibration at Work Regulations, is critical to prevent this. To help you manage and minimize the risk of HAVS, we have developed a free, easy-to-use HAVS Calculator. This tool helps track daily vibration exposure easily and accurately. Key Features of the HAVS Calculator

  1. Enter The Date

    Start by entering the date of the recording. This helps in maintaining an accurate timeline of tool usage and exposure levels.

  2. Person's Name

    Enter your name. This helps in tracking individual exposure records and maintaining personalized data for compliance and safety monitoring.

  3. Tool Name Entry

    Enter the name of the tool you're using. This allows you to track specific equipment over time and monitor exposure trends.

  4. Vibration Magnitude Rating

    Enter the vibration magnitude rating of the tool, measured in meters per second squared (m/s²). This rating can usually be found in the tool’s manual or technical specifications.

  5. Trigger Time

    Log the duration of exposure, also known as "trigger time." This is the time you spend actively operating the tool. Our calculator takes this input and generates an accurate estimate of exposure points.

  6. Automatic Point Calculation:

    Using the entered vibration magnitude and trigger time, the HAVs calculator automatically generates the total points for the task. Under UK law, the EAV Exposure Action Value (EAV) is set at 100 points, while the ELV Exposure Limit Value (ELV) is capped at 400 points. Our tool ensures that your exposure levels are always within legal limits.

  7. Combined Tool Use

    If you use several tools during the day, the calculator helps you combine them. This gives you a clear view of your total exposure. This is critical in ensuring that cumulative exposure remains below the legal threshold.

  8. Download Your Data

    After you enter all your tool information, vibration levels, and usage times, you can download a report. This report will summarize your exposure in detail. This feature helps in tracking and recording daily exposure for compliance, safety audits, and long-term monitoring.

The Dangers of Hand-Arm Vibration

Hand-Arm Vibration Syndrome (HAVS) doesn’t occur overnight. It develops over time through sustained exposure to vibrating tools such as power drills, chainsaws, grinders, and jackhammers. If left unmanaged, HAVS can cause:

  • Tingling or numbness in fingers and hands
  • Loss of grip strength
  • Permanent damage to blood circulation
  • Chronic pain and discomfort

The risks are avoidable with the right precautions in place. Our HAVs calculator is designed to give you an easy, effective solution to monitor and reduce vibration exposure, protecting your workforce from harm.
